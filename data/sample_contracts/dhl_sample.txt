DHL EXPRESS SERVICE TERMS 2024
Effective Date: February 1, 2024

SECTION 1: CLAIM FILING DEADLINES
Visible damage: 7 days from delivery
Concealed damage: 14 days from delivery
Late delivery claims: 30 days from ship date

SECTION 2: LIABILITY LIMITS
Automatic coverage: $100
Additional insurance: $1.50 per $100 declared value, up to $100,000

SECTION 3: PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS
Fragile items: Bubble wrap + "FRAGILE" labels required
Temperature-controlled: DHL Thermo packaging required
