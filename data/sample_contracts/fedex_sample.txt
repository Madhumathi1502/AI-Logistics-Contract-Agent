FEDEX GROUND SERVICE AGREEMENT 2024
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

SECTION 1: CLAIM FILING DEADLINES
Claims for visible damage must be filed within 7 calendar days of delivery.
Claims for concealed damage must be filed within 15 calendar days of delivery.
Claims for loss must be filed within 7 calendar days of expected delivery.

SECTION 2: LIABILITY LIMITS
Maximum liability for standard packages is $100 per package.
Declared value available up to $50,000 per package at additional cost.

SECTION 3: PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS
Fragile items require double-box packaging with minimum 2 inches of cushioning.
Items valued over $500 require signature confirmation.
Hazardous materials require DG declaration form.

SECTION 4: PICKUP COMMITMENTS
Pickup within 60 minutes of scheduled time guaranteed.
Late pickup penalty: $50 per occurrence.

SECTION 5: RATE DISPUTES
Rate disputes must be filed within 30 days of invoice date.
