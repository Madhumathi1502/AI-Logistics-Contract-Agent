UPS SERVICE AGREEMENT 2024
Effective Date: March 15, 2024

SECTION 1: CLAIM FILING DEADLINES
All claims must be filed within 14 calendar days of delivery.
Concealed damage claims must be filed within 14 days.

SECTION 2: LIABILITY LIMITS
Standard liability: $150 per package.
Additional insurance available up to $75,000.

SECTION 3: PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS
Fragile items require foam wrap and "FRAGILE" labels.
Signature required for items over $750.
